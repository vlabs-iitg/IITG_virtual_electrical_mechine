Objective

To measure the DC resistance of the stator of the induction motor.

Overview

The DC resistance test is performed to measure the resistance of the stator by circulating a suitable direct current and then measuring the voltage drop between terminals. The test, if taken with the motor cold (at room temperature) provides a check for the calculated values, or a basis for the estimation of efficiency.
